Hello, I'm Simo.

This a little summary of my work, process, and thoughts.

I'm an interaction + product designer based in Shanghai, where I consult for international clients and startups on present and future products with my studio antefact and where I research future relationships with technology as part of automato.farm

I teach about futures, connecivity, and intelligence at CIID, write about the home of the future with O'Reilly and sometimes speak at international conferences like Interaction17, Solid Con and TEDx.
Some of my work is exhibited internationally in galleries and museums such as Vitra Design Museum and awarded by IXDA Interaction Awards, IDSA IDEA Award, Core77 Awards and Fastco's Innovation by Design.
